My name is Edwin Senkumba, a freelance animator and 3D artist from Uganda. I am a passionate creative with over 5 years working experience in media production and element creation for animation and graphics. I have had the privilege to work on print, TV and online media projects for companies & brands like JESA Farm Dairy, Bank of Uganda, NATURA (Modern Dairy Burundi), NICE House of Plastics and Uganda Batteries Limited among others.

I am a self-taught artist driven by a tenacious curiosity that constantly motivates me to push my limits, improving myself as an artist. The combination of my technical background and my passion for art and design has given me the ability to be adaptive to new concepts and design situations with an eagerness to learn and evolve.

If you'd like to discuss your creative requirements then please don't hesitate to get in touch with me. Thank you for taking the time to view my portfolio. I do my best to regularly update it so feel free to visit again at any future time!

Have a great day!
